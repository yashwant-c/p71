  • COVID-19 World: Shanghai School Has Robot Chef In Canteen To Prepare, Serve Food

    The Minhang High School Experimental School, with 11-13 years old students, has employed an almost 10-feet tall robot in its canteen that uses a giant arm to pick up pre-prepared individual dishes and heat them in a microwave. The school claims that the new robot ensures that the food is not touched by humans during the whole process, ensuring safety of the students by eliminating secondary contact.




  • HARRIS. Art Academy to Open as Area's First Art School to Encourage Expressionism, Using Modern Media in New Art Education Approach
